Rockbuster Story

Overview

Rockbuster Stealth LLC is a movie rental company that used to have stores around the world. Currently, it is facing stiff competition from streaming services such as Netflix and Amazon Prime.


Objective

Planning to use its existing movie licenses to launch an online video rental service in order to stay competitive.

  • Project Scale: One month
  • Analysis based on the year: 2020
  • Top Sales Regions: Mexico, Philippines, Turkey

Key Questions:
  • - Which movies contributed the most/least to revenue gain?
  • - What was the average rental duration for all videos?
  • - Which countries are Rockbuster customers based in?
  • - Where are customers with a high lifetime value based?
  • - Do sales figures vary between geographic regions?

  • Records: 17 tables
  • Information: Rentals, film, customers, payment
  • Data Citation: Rockbuster dataset
    Last update: 02/15/2006






Recommendation

  • Rockbuster has 600 customers in 109 countries. It is a great opportunity to grow as an online service. Next step would be to introduce a subscription-based rental program; however, further analysis is required to plan to find the most effective monthly fee to beat the competitors.

  • To start the pilot service, we would recommend the top-3 revenue makers, which are the following countries: India, China, and the US.

  • We suggest the marketing team to provide promotions within popular countries with highest number of customers; India, China, the United States, Japan, and Mexico.

Challenges and Limitations

Complicated database structure and dependencies which lead to complex SQL queries which are prone to error.
